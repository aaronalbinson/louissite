MEXICAN FOOD INSTRUCTIONS!!!!!!

  • Enchiladas (feeds two)

ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion peeled and finely sliced
  • 1 red pepper de-seeded and finely sliced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 clove carlic finely chopped
  • 1/2 red chilli, de-seededand finely sliced or 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 co-op ready cooked chicken breasts, finely sliced
  • handful chooped criander (optional)
  • 4 tortilla wraps
  • 300g tub tomato and basil sauce
  • 50g greated cheese

method

1. Heat the oil in a wok or frying pan, and gentely fry the onion and pepper for a few minutes.

2. Add the garlic, ground coriander, garlic and chilli, and fry for 1 minute.

3. aaadd the chicken, half of the fresh corriander, if using. mix togeather.

4. spon a quater of the mix into the tortilla wrap, fold each end, and roll up.

5. Place in an overproof dish and do the same with the rest of the mix and tortilla wraps.

6. Pour the tomato and basil sauce over the wraps, sprinkle over with cheese and bake for 20 minutes, until bubling hot.

7. Sprinkle with more corriander if you like.
